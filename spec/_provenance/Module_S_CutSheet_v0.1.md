## §1 Verdict & counts

**Module S is the commerce engine — and the FIRST genuinely cut-heavy module of the triage.** The consumer core-loop floor stays whole (browse → buy → pay; sanctions/Hold gate; tax-correct INV1/INV2/INV3; the no-overselling hold/issuance surface; 1-voucher-per-bottle; the Voucher FSM), the club value-proposition stays whole (Hero Package → Club Credit → redeem; the membership purchase loop; stacking spine), but **four real Module-S-layer cuts land here** — two forwarded from upstream (D7 + D8) and two in-module (D5 + D6). The supply-side quartet (0/K/A/D) each forwarded or pre-factored its headline lever and netted ~0 at its own layer; **Module S is where the deferred levers actually come to ground.** Six facts shape the verdict:

1. **D7 (the multi-producer composite cut forwarded from Module A) lands here — and it is the first real net-new Module-layer DEFER of the triage.** The substantive construct is entirely Module S's: ONE composite Offer reading N constituent Allocations atomically (DEC-097, §6), the atomic N-way decrement + atomic-rollback at voucher issuance (DEC-097 §6.2 + DEC-179), the composite mid-life cascade (§6.3), the 5-rule publication extension across N constituents (§7.2), and the composite OC-on-headline-`P_d` rule (§6.4). **DEFER the multi-producer composite construct; SHIP single-producer Offers** — club mixed-cases (single-producer by DEC-019) + single-Allocation Discovery Offers + the multi-Offer-per-Allocation multi-granularity pattern (DEC-099) all ship unchanged. **Seam (P1):** the Offer entity keeps the capacity to reference N constituents later (`composite_constituent_allocation_ids[]` ships in single-FK form; the multi-FK *binding logic* is the additive part); Module A keeps the per-constituent single-Allocation + two-FK + per-constituent `commercial_terms` (ratified); Composite SKU retained at Module 0 (ratified). **This is a Paolo business call — Discovery is a business pillar** (Q1).

2. **D8 (the Club-Credit / stacking savings-hunt forwarded from Module K) lands here — and the honest finding is that the club VP is load-bearing, so the safe savings are modest and clean.** KEEP the club VP whole (Club Credit entity redemption + auto-apply DEC-111; the stacking spine DEC-110; closure-conversion DEC-043). The genuine, clean deferrals are the two *peripheral* K-forwarded mechanics — **K.18 welcome-window proportional scaling (DEFER)** and **K.19 operator manual Club-Credit issuance (DEFER — route launch goodwill through the single REFUND_COMPENSATION coupon instrument)**. The third K-forwarded mechanic — **K.17 partial-redemption + carry-forward (KEEP)** — is examined and found **load-bearing with real customer-value risk if cut** (members spend annual credit across several purchases); recommending KEEP *against* the K-draft's tentative "defer all three." The 7-step stacking chain is v17-inherited and is the seam; the only launch *simplification* is which discount interactions are configured (Q3) — a thin, mostly-config posture, honestly reported.

3. **D5 (gifting) is a clean in-module DEFER.** Gifting (DEC-116, §13) is not in the core loop. DEFER the GIFTED voucher state + the 7-day accept flow + the recipient-gate validation + the `VoucherGift*` event family (4 events). **Voucher FSM collapses 8 → 7 states** at launch. **Seam (P1):** preserve the Voucher's ownership-transfer capability (the customer-reference is mutable — no hard single-permanent-owner assumption), so member-to-member gifting is an additive post-launch build; the Originating-Club-preservation hook rides on the kept Voucher `originating_club_id`.

4. **D6 (cancellation/refund) is an in-module SIMPLIFY that keeps the legal floor whole.** KEEP the 14-day pre-shipment window from INV1 (DEC-108, legal floor), the post-shipment WAIVER (Article 16 carve-out), per-voucher partial refund (DEC-109), refund-at-original-FX, the OC-reversal (`DiscoveryRevenueShareReversed`), and the `VoucherVoided` → Module D PI-cancel unwind. **SIMPLIFY the refund-cost-matrix sophistication** (DEC-025 cause-routing, producer-fault clawback netting, DEC-044 goodwill-105% decisioning) → manual-first operator handling; the producer-fault clawback netting is deferred-with-settlement anyway (D19). Mechanisms KEPT; *decisioning* manual. Engineering-default (Q5).

5. **Module S owns the load-bearing floor on the consumer side — verified whole.** The **sanctions/Hold gate at order completion (DEC-113, §10.1/10.2)** is THE compliance enforcement point (Module K is sanctions-blind; Module A is sanctions-blind; the floor fires HERE). **Tax-correct INV1/INV2/INV3** under the MPV VAT regime (DEC-107/112/118/119). The **no-overselling hold/issuance surface** — Cart Hold strict-timeout (DEC-106), multi-Offer shared-pool decrement (DEC-099), the lesser-of storefront ATP (Layer 1 Module A pool ∧ Layer 2 Module B physical, §17.3 / DEC-185/187), 1-voucher-per-bottle (DEC-109). The **Voucher FSM** (the artefact the customer owns). **None is cut**; these compose with Module A's Layer 1 + Module B's Layer 2.

6. **Two contract-consistency reconciliations + one event-naming discharge land here.** (a) The three Module-D-owed Wave-3 event names resolve to **existing Module S events** — no net-new events (Q6): the V1/V2 PI auto-fire trigger is **`VoucherIssued`** (AMB-D-5, §17.4); the sell-through/PO-ownership-transition signal is **also `VoucherIssued`** (AMB-D-3 — there is NO separate `SellThroughRecorded` event; `VoucherIssued` is the sell-through signal, with `VoucherShipped` available for the shipment-keyed leg); the PI-cancel signal is **`VoucherVoided`** (D.28). (b) The MVP PRD must **RECONCILE BR-S-CrossModule-4** (PRD §18.16 still carries the stale DEC-118 "bidirectional Module S ↔ Module E at INV2" framing; DEC-119 made storage Module-S-internal — single Module D → Module S read; the §14 body and the Acceptance doc already carry the correct framing) — exactly mirroring Module D's DEC-183 reconciliation (Q7).

The L-PP treatment is identical to Module A's and Module D's: **Module S retains ZERO producer writes** at launch. Club Offer publication + Hero Package designation are producer/back-office writes → operator-driven via the Admin Panel; Discovery Offers are already Admin-Panel-only (DEC-115). DEC-115/083 parity is a backend contract, so **no backend capability is cut**. **The consumer storefront is EXEMPT (§3 of the kickoff):** browse / buy / cart / checkout / cellar stay self-serve (Q8).

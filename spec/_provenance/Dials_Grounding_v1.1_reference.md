## Heaviest-looking at launch (prime-cut targets)

1. **Inventory authority (Stocktake/quarantine/adjustments, D16)** — entire Module B layer is Stage-8 net-new; largest single increment; bidirectional with A/D/E, read by C/S.
2. **Club / membership model (D8)** — spans K (profiles/OC), S (Hero + 7-step stacking + Club Credit), A (capacity), E (OC share); foundational, touches eligibility everywhere.
3. **Cancellation/refund + Returns/replacement (D6+D14)** — two interlocking post-sale workflows, high invariant density, many cross-module event chains.
4. **Geography + customs/excise/DDP-DAP (D3)** — ~25 countries incl. US/OFAC, bonded VAT, excise event, two-tier eligibility; heavy compliance even at the "simple" baseline.
5. **NFT/blockchain + NFC (D12)** — Avalanche mint/burn lifecycle, custodial wallet, cross-module burn chain, 4 recovery scenarios; carries the working-hypothesis/feature-flag risk + external-review gate.
6. **Supplier settlement + multi-currency dual-recording (D19+D18)** — intricate Module E mechanics feeding Xero with strict immutability/credit-note discipline.
7. **Late-binding pick algorithm (D13)** — two-surface selection within pool boundary, serialized vs non-serialized paths; load-bearing fulfilment primitive in the highest-density module.

*Structural note:* the three-actor invoicing split (S=WHEN / Xero=WHAT / E=HOW) and allocation lineage are the most load-bearing cross-cutting patterns; they tax dials 6, 14, 18, 19, 22 and constrain how cheaply any can be cut.
